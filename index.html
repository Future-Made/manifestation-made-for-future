Extending the Freedom of Expression

with Expression of Demand

This umbrella project intends to propose a demand-augmented framework/concept for the enablement of making the outputs of future decision-making processes more foreseeable, while enabling (almost) self-promoting & incentivising activities and relationships-making, easier for individuals and groups in the tangible nature of declaring or hiding intentions and actions on the future resolutions.

Art Events @ Demand-Augmented Framework

Say, all ads
.. rather than conventional app/browser level ad-blockers, at once. Then what?

Creating a back-pressure to the "forced" energy consumption cross devices and apps, and "context switch" requirements on human mind; originating from those platforms' and conventional ways of getting insights focusing on "impressions", rather than "intentions".

Both consumers and producers are losing from current targeting and interaction methods.

Independency and self-sufficiency of many organisation of good cause, depend on such income by hosting those ads as well; along with producers to continue their production.

We are at the end, blindly closing ourselves to ads and products via existing blockers, required to manage advertisement blockings based on per-site, ad server/cluster etc.

It could be great to enable consumers to build and share a community-driven demand-side-platform, that is initiated by the demand-side itself and including artists, SMEs and NGOs as well, rather than its conventional definition. then they would keep their anonymity on individual level, until when they are reached by producers/brands, until there is a need to expose themselves, eg. transaction/logistics of the goods & services.

With this approach, variety of identities (individual consumer, consumer as household and as larger community) can further filter or allow ads, offers and special quotes based on their needs or increase the possibility for access at individual levels this way, hearing from offers given to larger groups, so no one is specifically needed/allowed being monitored and a collective real-time demand timeline there, is a collectively fuzzy entity of needs, preferences and values; in terms of anonymity of those.

Consumers/audiences help reducing costs, being part of sponsor of costs, automatically (ad/promotion and other costs, eg. listed for an event.)


We call this demand-casting.


Relevancy Filters, Browsing Environment | Time/Frequency and Media Type Filters on Ads

Introducing ethical ads and
intentionally visit and browse-ability & individually and collectively curate-ability and of those multimedia/advertisement networks,

A single point of truth that could be managed by consumer side, to filter ads based on their declared needs, future interest or oppositely, a detachment from categories of commodity/services, producer/brands, advertisers


####### Examples #######
Filter out brand-X, until they adapt fair practices | Allow food ads only for vegetarian/vegan food | Allow car ads only for electrical cars | Show me no cars at all



People cannot decide what, when, where and for how long to be exposed to an advertisement. There is no general, convenient and efficient setting/enablement for such actions.

Devices are consuming both internet/data usage/bandwidth; and making those to consume more energy in the name of a prediction. It is not only the energy consumption of those advertising multi- media to be considered as "waste", it is also the data usage limit (eg. as in gigabytes, activated and renewed for a monthly basis) that people pays to their internet service providers, being wasted and unseemly leaking from users' pockets, in the form of energy and data bandwidth.

In the established, coarse grained multimedia serving datacenters scenario (as-is), all multimedia streaming is required to be served to everywhere from central points, even if it might conveniently and efficiently available and accessible to be consumed/transferred/streamed within a range, with more locality. (problem for all)

The whole logic of relying on predictions and this abstraction layer and staying "not actively augmented by the demand" is causing a difficult decision making on the estimations for the producers' capacity planning for the goods/services they are opt for providing.

As a result of all those above, people are going away from the advertisements to feel less exposed to surveillance and malicious advertising (aka. malvertising).

That's how content creators and all parties benefiting from displaying an ad gets financially and reputation-wise in risk of losing their such active/passive income they would gain.

The revenues that creatives/artists really deserve are first and directly collected only by the platform provider(s). However, there is more serious issue to that. their artwork/content can be affiliated/served along with politically manipulative content, targeted ad(!)s.

As a result of all of this, there is an abstract, ambiguous, huge blinded layer of "unknown knowns"; which are leading to hesitation, mistrust and common questioning through the society.
