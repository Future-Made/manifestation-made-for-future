“Expression of Demand”

This umbrella project, “FutureMade” introduces a demand-augmented framework for the enablement of making future more clearer for tangible things, by making use of “a time period ahead” to gather real event and content demands from audiences of the artists, journalists and scientists through incentivizing demand-ability of their creations and ideas, for the sake of the betterment on their accessibility and sustainability of the arts, culture and knowledge.

Our motivation and expectation focuses around building an initial set of enabler platforms that aims to fairly connect the artists and journalists with their existing and potential audiences, SMEs* (Places| Radios) and NGOs**, along with other parties*** that would be integrated in the future.

A Comparison:

For any participant with the audience behaviour, effective mechanisms “Expression of Demand” will be enabled, hence encouraged and empowered at wherever possible; augmented via "tolerable" levels of transparency and anonymity, for the sake of being able to freely intend to create socio-economical relations and incentives making, in a multi-lateral manner.

Mikey Goodfire Musician
He’s trying to capture the sound in his head from things that inspired him and are now creatively expanding into something unique! His name is Mikey Goodfire and he’s from The Netherlands. Ranging from melancholic acoustic guitar compositions to progressive electronic tracks, they all have one thing in common.. Groove!! The sound overall is catchy and pleasant to the ear and the electronic tracks often have a provocative psychedelic but chill vibe. He grew up in The Netherlands in a family where both parents played music, he quickly started playing a wide range of instruments. Moving abroad to Denmark in early adolescence he joined a band.. A rockband!! That band was called The Black Leaves where he held his spot for total of 6 years as a bass player making it onto three albums. Suddenly he felt the urge to pick up the guitar and start writing his own material having earned enough confidence to do so. It didn’t take long before he isolated himself in the studio, long nights and the weekends finding his way and learning by doing. Some years later, he has now written some according to his audience, near masterpieces! Intricate works of art inspired by personal experiences, human nature and mind altering drugs, emotions.. a reflective subjection of his mind. Now he’s currently busy recording, producing and publishing his music with a music label.
